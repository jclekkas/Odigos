#18 - Final UX Polish: Structured Header + Article Improvements

What & Why
A focused layout and typography pass to resolve the remaining usability gaps identified after the Task #17 polish release. Goals: a proper structured page header (logo + nav), breadcrumb navigation on article pages, readable prose width, less cramped mobile example buttons, a clear explainer above the analyzer textarea, and a subtle methodology link that makes the product feel like serious documentation.

Done looks like
Every relevant page has a consistent full-width SiteHeader with the Odigos logo on the left and two nav links on the right ("Scenario Hub" → /dealer-pricing-problems, "How It Works" → /#how-it-works)

The logo sits inside a visually grounded header container and links to /

Article pages show a breadcrumb trail:
Home / Dealer Pricing Problems / [Article title]
The last crumb is plain text, not a link.

Article prose is constrained to max-w-[700px]

Analyzer example buttons stack vertically on mobile and sit in a row on desktop

"What This Deal Likely Means" narrative block is constrained to max-w-[750px] on large monitors

No changes to analyzer logic, scoring, prompts, Stripe flow, pricing, or routing
