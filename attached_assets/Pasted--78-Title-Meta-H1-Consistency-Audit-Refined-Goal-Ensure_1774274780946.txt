#78 - Title / Meta / H1 Consistency Audit (Refined)

## Goal

Ensure every page has a unique, keyword-targeted title, a matching H1, and a distinct meta description. Eliminate keyword cannibalization and strengthen ranking signals.

---

## Global Rules (STRICT)

1. **One primary keyword per page**

   * Each page must clearly target a different search intent
   * No two pages should target the same core query

2. **Title must lead with the primary keyword**

   * The most important phrase must appear at the beginning of the title

3. **Title uniqueness**

   * No two titles may share the same first 4 words (stricter than before)

4. **H1 alignment**

   * H1 must closely match the title’s primary keyword
   * Natural variation is allowed, but intent must be identical

5. **Meta descriptions**

   * Must be unique across all pages
   * Must reflect the page’s distinct angle (not generic summaries)

6. **Structured data sync**

   * Any `articleSchema(...)` must use EXACTLY the same title and description as `setSeoMeta`
   * No stale strings allowed

7. **No content/body edits**

   * Only modify title, H1, description, and schema
