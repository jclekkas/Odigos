#78 - Title / Meta / H1 Consistency Audit (Final)

## Goal

Ensure every page has a unique, keyword-targeted title, a matching H1, and a distinct meta description. Eliminate keyword cannibalization and strengthen ranking + click-through rate (CTR).

---

## Global Rules (STRICT)

1. **One primary keyword per page**

   * Each page must target a distinct search intent
   * No two pages may target the same core query

2. **Title must lead with the primary keyword**

   * The most important phrase must appear at the beginning

3. **Title uniqueness**

   * No two titles may share the same first 4 words

4. **H1 alignment**

   * H1 must closely match the title’s primary keyword
   * Natural variation allowed, but intent must be identical

5. **Meta descriptions (REQUIRED)**

   * Must be fully written, concrete strings (140–160 characters)
   * Must be unique across all pages
   * Must reflect the page’s distinct angle
   * Do NOT leave description generation to interpretation

6. **CTR optimization**

   * Titles must be written to maximize click-through rate
   * Use specificity, numbers, contrast, or implied risk where appropriate

7. **Structured data sync**

   * Any `articleSchema(...)` must use EXACTLY the same title and description as `setSeoMeta`
   * No stale strings allowed

8. **No content/body edits**

   * Only modify title, H1, description, and schema
