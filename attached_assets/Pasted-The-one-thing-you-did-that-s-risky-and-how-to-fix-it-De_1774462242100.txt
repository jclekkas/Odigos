The one thing you did that’s risky (and how to fix it)
⚠️ Dealer name extraction via regex pre-LLM
“Dealer name is extracted from raw input text using a regex heuristic”
This is the weakest link in the system now.
Why it matters:
Your entire feedback loop depends on correct dealer attribution
Regex will silently fail or mis-assign:
“Toyota of Rockville” vs “Rockville Toyota”
Missing dealer names in screenshots
Multiple dealers in a thread
Typos / formatting differences
What to do next (not urgent, but important)
Short-term (easy win):
Log a dealer_match_confidence or boolean like:
isDealerMatched: true/false
Only apply feedback signal when match is high-confidence
Medium-term (high ROI):
Move dealer extraction into the LLM itself (structured output)
You already trust it for analysis — this is easier
Or normalize dealer names into a canonical table (fuzzy matching)
Right now, this is the only part that can quietly poison your feedback loop.
Subtle product insight you just unlocked (this is big)

Ground truth disagreement between your model and real users
That’s rare.
Most AI products never get this.

1. Calibration over time (huge)

“Are we too harsh on YELLOW?”
“Are RED scores actually trusted?”
“Which issues users disagree with most?”
