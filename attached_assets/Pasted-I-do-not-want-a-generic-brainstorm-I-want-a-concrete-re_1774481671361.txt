Evaluate whether Spanish-language pages are worth building, and if so, exactly how to do it in a high-leverage, low-waste way.

Rules:
- Do not give vague “it depends” advice
- Do not suggest translating the whole site

- Be blunt

- likely search intent

9. 30-day plan
