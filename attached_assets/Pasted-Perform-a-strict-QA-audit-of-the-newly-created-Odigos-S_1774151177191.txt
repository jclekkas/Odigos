Perform a strict QA audit of the newly created Odigos SEO article pages.

CRITICAL CONTEXT:
These pages are part of tightly structured SEO clusters (OTD pricing and dealer add-ons). Each page is intended to target a distinct search intent and must NOT overlap meaningfully with others.

Your job is NOT to rewrite everything. Your job is to IDENTIFY and FIX issues that would weaken SEO performance, including duplication, weak differentiation, incorrect internal linking, or poor metadata.

---

PAGES TO AUDIT:
(List all newly created pages here)

---

AUDIT REQUIREMENTS:

1. PAGE DIFFERENTIATION (MOST IMPORTANT)

For each page:

* Summarize its core intent in ONE sentence
* Compare it against the other pages in the same cluster
* Identify ANY overlap in:
  • explanations
  • structure
  • repeated phrases
  • reused sections

If overlap >20%:

* Flag it clearly
* Rewrite ONLY the overlapping sections to make the page distinct

---

2. DUPLICATION & AI PATTERN DETECTION

Check across all pages for:

* repeated sentence structures
* similar paragraph openings
* reused explanation blocks
* templated-feeling sections

If found:

* Rewrite those sections to sound natural and distinct
* Do NOT rewrite entire pages — only fix the patterns

---

3. INTERNAL LINKING VALIDATION

For each page:

* List all internal links used
* Verify each slug exists and matches App.tsx exactly
* Ensure:
  • at least 2–3 internal links per page
  • anchor text is natural and varied
  • no overuse of exact-match anchors

Fix any:

* incorrect slugs
* missing links
* repetitive anchors

---

4. CROSS-CLUSTER LINKING

Ensure:

* OTD pages link to add-ons pages where relevant
* Add-ons pages link back to OTD pages where relevant

If missing:

* Add 1–2 natural cross-cluster links per page

---

5. CTA PLACEMENT & QUALITY

For each page:

* Identify where the mid-article CTA appears
* Confirm it is placed after a strong insight (not arbitrarily)

Check:

* CTA wording is not identical across all pages
* It feels natural in context

If needed:

* Adjust placement or wording slightly

---

6. SEO METADATA CHECK

For each page:

* Title:
  • under 60 characters
  • includes target keyword naturally
  • ends with "| Odigos"

* Meta description:
  • 140–155 characters
  • specific to the page (not generic)
  • includes a mild action signal

Fix anything that is:

* too long
* too vague
* repetitive across pages

---

7. FINAL QUALITY SCORE

For each page:

* Rate from 1–10 on:
  • differentiation
  • clarity
  • usefulness
  • SEO readiness

If any page is below 8:

* Explain why
* Fix the issue

---

OUTPUT FORMAT:

For each page:

* Issues found (if any)
* Fixes applied
* Final score

At the end:

* Summary of overall cluster quality
* Any systemic issues across pages

---

GOAL:

These pages should:

* feel like distinct, standalone articles
* work together as a cohesive SEO cluster
* avoid duplication or templated patterns
* be strong enough to rank independently
