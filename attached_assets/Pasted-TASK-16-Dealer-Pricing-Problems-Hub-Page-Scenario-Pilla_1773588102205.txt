TASK 16 — Dealer Pricing Problems Hub Page (Scenario Pillar)

What & Why

Odigos now has 15 scenario pages covering specific dealership situations (price changes, missing quotes, finance office tactics, add-ons, etc.).

These pages are linked from guides and articles, but there is no single page that organizes them into a clear topic cluster.

Creating a hub page improves:

• SEO topical authority (pillar → cluster structure)  
• Crawl discoverability for scenario pages  
• User navigation during stressful dealership situations  
• Internal linking density across the scenario network  

This page should function as a navigation and authority page, not a long article.
