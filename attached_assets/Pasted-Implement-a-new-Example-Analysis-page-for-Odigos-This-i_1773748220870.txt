Implement a new “Example Analysis” page for Odigos.

This is a standalone, read-only page designed to show users exactly what Odigos output looks like on a realistic dealer quote.

---

OBJECTIVE

Increase trust and conversion by making the product tangible.

Users should be able to:

* see a realistic dealer message
* see how Odigos analyzes it
* understand what issues are detected
* understand what they would do next

This page is NOT interactive.
This page is NOT connected to backend logic.

All content is static and hardcoded.

---

ROUTE

Create a new route:

/example-analysis

Register it in client routing (App.tsx)

---

PAGE STRUCTURE

1. HEADER

Title:
“Example: How Odigos analyzes a dealer quote”

Subtext:
“Here’s a realistic example of a dealer message and what Odigos flags before you walk into the dealership.”

---

2. DEALER MESSAGE BLOCK

Label:
“Dealer message”

Display a realistic message in a styled container (muted background, rounded):

"Hi, the vehicle is $28,995. With taxes, fees, and protection package you're looking at $34,200 OTD. Monthly comes out to about $540 depending on credit. Let me know when you can come in."

---

3. ODIGOS RESULT BLOCK

Label:
“Odigos analysis”

Include:

Verdict:
“⚠️ Proceed with caution”

Section: “Key issues found”

• No itemized breakdown of fees
• “Protection package” not explained or optional
• Focus on monthly payment instead of total cost

Section: “Why this matters”

Short paragraph explaining:
This structure can hide add-ons, inflate total cost, and shift focus away from the real out-the-door price.

---

4. WHAT TO DO NEXT

Label:
“What to say back”

Provide a copy-paste response:

"Can you provide a full itemized breakdown of the out-the-door price, including all fees and add-ons?"

---

5. CTA SECTION

At the bottom:

“Have your own dealer quote?”

Primary CTA button:
“Check a Dealer Quote” → /analyze
